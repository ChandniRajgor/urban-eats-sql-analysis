# 📊 UrbanEats Business Performance & Customer Behavior Analysis (SQL)

This project analyzes a food delivery platform dataset to uncover insights on revenue trends, customer behavior, and restaurant performance using SQL.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

UrbanEats lacked visibility into customer behavior, revenue trends, and restaurant performance.  
The goal of this analysis was to identify high-value customers, popular regions, and key business drivers using transactional data.

---

## ⚙️ Approach

- Built relational understanding across orders, customers, restaurants, and meals  
- Calculated platform revenue and restaurant revenue  
- Analyzed customer behavior and ordering patterns  
- Identified top-performing restaurants, categories, and meals  
- Detected loyal and high-value customers  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue Trends
- Total revenue: **₹6.06L (6 months)**  
- Average monthly revenue: **~₹1L**  
- Slight increase in February and April, with a dip in March → possible seasonal trend  

---

### 👥 Customer Behavior
- **20.5% of customers drive the majority of revenue** (high concentration)  
- Most users stay within budget → **price-sensitive audience**  
- Average: **2 meals per order**, which can help optimize delivery pricing strategies  

---

### 🍽️ Restaurant & Market Insights
- Top-performing restaurants: **13, 30, 18, 20**  
- Budget-friendly restaurants: **3, 28**  
- Premium restaurants: **11, 10, 4**  
- High-demand regions: **Herzelia, Ramat Hasharon**  
- Popular categories: **Fast Food, Asian, Indian**  

👉 These insights can improve the platform’s recommendation system  

---

### ⏰ Ordering Patterns
- Peak hours:
  - Lunch: **11 AM – 1 PM**
  - Dinner: **7 PM – 9 PM**
- Opportunity to increase revenue by promoting **off-peak meals**  

---

### 🥗 Food Trends
- Popular: **Cheese & Vegan meals**  
- Premium: **Chicken meals**  
- Average order value: **~87 ILS**  

---

### 👩‍🦰👨 Gender Insights
- **Ramat Hasharon** → more popular among female customers  
- **Tel Aviv** → more popular among male customers  
- **Herzelia** → balanced preference  

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

- Target high-value customers with personalized offers and loyalty programs  
- Encourage higher spending through discounts and bundled offers  
- Promote off-peak meals (breakfast, appetizers) to increase revenue  
- Use gender and location insights for personalized marketing campaigns  
- Partner more with top-performing restaurants to maximize growth  
- Recommend restaurants based on customer preferences and occasions (weekends, special events)
